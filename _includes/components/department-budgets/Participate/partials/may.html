What are Treasury, Departments and Parliament busy with?

  • National Treasury issues budget guidelines to departments requesting budget submissions, including their 3-year expenditure estimates, in accordance with the adopted budget strategy and planned budget process.
  • Departments engage with analysts on departmental budgets and the drafting of budget submissions.
  • Parliament reviews each department's budget, including the department's Annual Performance Plan (APP) and Strategic Plan (SP).

How can I participate?

  • Review the department's Annual Annual Performance Plans (APPs) and Strategic Plans (SPs).
  • Engage government departments and relevant committees on the content of the APPs and SPs and whether departmental budgets have been properly prioritised.
